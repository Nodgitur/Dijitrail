That Sinking Feeling

The thought behind the website

This page based on the German Progressive Metal band The Ocean. They have been releasing periodic albums based on various sections of the universe since 2000. Including the layers of the sea, to a journey of the heliocentric human view. Most recently they have released an album on the formation of Earth, going through millions of years as the songs progress. This is the first part of the "Phanerozoic" series, with the second part aimed to be released in 2020.

After finding out about this band earlier this year, I was engrossed in it's creative naturistic work that was an open canvis for a webpage. For the home page of the website I had chosen the album Pelagial. An album that has a objective of taking the listener into the downward zones of the sea, hence the gradient. The music brings heavier tones as it demonstrates the more claustrophobic atmosphere of pressure, and diminishing light that the deeper depths would bring on a diver. It was also meant as an extended metaphor for the mind of a psyche
